## 🇺🇸 English Version

SSM DRMGL is a personal hobby project developed in my free time.  
Your support helps keep the project alive and motivates future updates.

There are two ways to support the project:

---

## 1. Support via Google Play (In‑App Support)

The Android app provides optional in‑app support using **Google Play Billing**.  
Three support tiers are available:

- **support_small** – Small contribution  
- **support_medium** – Medium contribution  
- **support_large** – Large contribution  

All support options are completely optional and do not unlock any exclusive features.  
They simply help maintain development, testing, and future protocol research.

### Technical Notes (for developers)
- BillingClient is initialized on app startup  
- SupportBillingManager handles purchase flow  
- A toast message is shown on successful support  
- Internal testing can be done via Google Play Console (License Tester)

---

## 2. Support via BuyMeACoffee (External Link)

If you prefer supporting outside the Play Store,  
you can contribute any amount through BuyMeACoffee:

👉 https://buymeacoffee.com/ssmdrmglapq

This option allows flexible support amounts and works on any device.

## 1. Google Play からのサポート（アプリ内サポート）

Android アプリでは **Google Play Billing** を利用した任意サポートが可能です。  
以下の 3 種類のサポート項目があります：

- **support_small** – 小額サポート  
- **support_medium** – 中額サポート  
- **support_large** – 大額サポート  

サポート額によって機能差はありません。  
開発・テスト・車両プロトコル研究の継続に役立ちます。

### 技術メモ（開発者向け）
- BillingClient は起動時に初期化  
- SupportBillingManager が購入処理を管理  
- サポート成功時に Toast を表示  
- Google Play Console のライセンステスターで動作確認可能
